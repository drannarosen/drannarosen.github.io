The Chapter 8 tool, and the playable core of *Confidently Wrong*. **Binaries on/off** on
the cluster the reader built: watch unresolved pairs blend, then re-fit the IMF slope from
the *observed* cluster and see it drift from the truth — and get **more precisely wrong**
as N grows.

**Toggles:** binaries on/off · binary fraction · rotation on/off · N.
**Data:** photometric blending + naive-vs-binary-aware IMF fit. **Research:** Confidently
Wrong (binaries bias IMF inference at large N). **Deep dive:** binary evolution → GW mergers.

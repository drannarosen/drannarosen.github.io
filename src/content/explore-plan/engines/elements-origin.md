The Chapter 7 tool and the outreach centerpiece. Step the cluster forward and watch
elements light up as its stars live and die; hover an element to see its dominant origin
(Big Bang, AGB winds, core-collapse SNe, neutron-star mergers); watch metals accumulate in
the gas.

**Toggles:** time · highlight-by-origin.
**Data:** illustrative nucleosynthetic-origin fractions (standard compilation, cited).
**Honesty:** a teaching visualization of dominant channels, not a yield calculation.

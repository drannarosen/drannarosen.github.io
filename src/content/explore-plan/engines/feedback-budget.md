The Chapter 9 tool, and Anna's research made tactile. Toggle each channel — outflows,
winds, radiation pressure, photoionization, supernovae — and watch an **energy/momentum
ledger** (injected vs retained vs radiated/leaked) alongside an **expanding wind bubble
and HII region** carving into the gas. Dial the **coupling efficiency** and watch the gas
either blow out or stay bound, compared against the cloud's binding energy.

## Reuse — the gravoturb gas engine

The bubbles and ionization fronts expand into **the same gravoturbulent gas dynamics
already built for the Birth story** (the **progenax** density field the hero/scrolly
engine renders) — so feedback acts on *real turbulent gas*, not a blank canvas, and we
build one gas engine, not two. This is the reuse discipline the whole series depends on.

**Toggles:** each channel on/off · coupling efficiency · cloud binding energy.
**Data:** per-channel budgets vs binding energy; HARM²-informed coupling; ORION2-informed
bubble/HII structure over the progenax gas field. **Honesty:** illustrative ledger and
expansion, not a live RHD run.

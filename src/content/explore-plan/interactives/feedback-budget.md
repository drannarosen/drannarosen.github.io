The Chapter 9 tool, and Anna's research made tactile. Toggle each channel — outflows,
winds, radiation pressure, photoionization, supernovae — and watch an **energy/momentum
ledger** (injected vs retained vs radiated/leaked) and the bubble respond. Dial the
**coupling efficiency** and watch the gas either blow out or stay bound, compared against
the cloud's binding energy.

**Toggles:** each channel on/off · coupling efficiency · cloud binding energy.
**Data:** per-channel budgets vs binding energy; HARM²-informed coupling. **Research:**
HARM²; the 30 Dor "lost X-ray energy" result. **Honesty:** illustrative ledger, not a live RHD run.

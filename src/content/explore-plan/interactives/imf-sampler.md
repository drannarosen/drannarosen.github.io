The Chapter 2 tool. Draw a cluster from the IMF and **bin it into a mass histogram**;
drag the high-mass slope **α** and the sample size **N** and watch the noisy histogram
converge (or not) on the underlying law, the heavy tail jumping most.

**Toggles:** α slope · N · (later) show/hide the true law.
**Data:** Kroupa sampler from `imf.ts` (built). **Research:** progenax IMF; the
sampled-distribution seed of Confidently Wrong.

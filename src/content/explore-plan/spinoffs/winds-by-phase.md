Deep dive off Chapter 5 (the one Anna flagged as closest to her heart). Follow a massive
star and watch its **wind change with phase** — hot main-sequence line-driving, the
cooler high-mass-loss giant/LBV/WR stages — and *why* each regime differs. Ties the
mass-loss history to the eventual remnant and to the feedback budget. Data: phase-resolved
wind prescriptions on startrax tracks.

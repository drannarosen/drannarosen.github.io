The opening act, and the reader's first act of creation: they set the initial
conditions and watch a cluster switch on.

## Story beat

Cold, turbulent molecular gas piles up under gravity; the densest knots collapse and
ignite; ten thousand stars appear, sampled from an initial mass function and placed by
the density they formed in. A handful of massive stars — born deepest — light up, and
their feedback drives the leftover gas outward until the cloud clears and a bare
cluster remains. This is the existing "Birth of a cluster" scrollytelling, promoted to
Chapter 1 of the series.

## What the reader does

Set the cloud and the **IMF slope α**; ignite; then push the **gas-expulsion** control
and watch the natal gas unbind. (Full IMF play lives in the linked tool; here it is a
taste.)

## The research, woven in

- The natal gas field is a **real gravoturbulent density field from progenax**, sculpted
  into filaments and a dense central clump by supersonic turbulence — not a decorative
  cloud.
- Gas expulsion is driven by massive-star radiation and winds — **the radiation-
  hydrodynamics Anna's HARM² and feedback work model**. This is the site's home turf.
- The IMF the reader sets here is the *ground truth* the whole series will later try to
  recover — the seed of the **Confidently Wrong** thread.

The cluster's portrait, and the reader's first real instrument.

## Story beat

Place every star by temperature and luminosity and the **main sequence** appears — a
diagonal from faint red dwarfs to blazing blue giants. The mass histogram of Chapter 2
and this diagram are **two projections of the same sampled population**; the upper-left
handful of hot stars, rare as they are, dominate the light and write the story that
follows.

## What the reader does

Hover or tap any star for its real properties (mass, spectral type, Teff, L, radius,
lifetime, fate); watch its point light up on the HR diagram. Change α and see the whole
diagram breathe.

## The research, woven in

- Every property is **derived from `stellar.ts`, ported from and validated against
  startrax** (Tout 1996, Hurley 2000, Heger 2003) — the same relations Anna's stellar
  code owns.
- The population's masses/positions carry the **progenax** IMF DNA of Chapter 2.

The end of the star you've followed since Chapter 4 — and the moment it becomes a source
that reshapes the whole cluster.

## Story beat

How a star dies is written at birth by its mass. Below ~8 M⊙ it puffs off its envelope
and leaves a **white dwarf**. Above that, the core collapses and the star explodes as a
**supernova** — leaving a **neutron star**, or, for the heaviest, a **black hole**. Each
core-collapse dumps ~10⁵¹ erg and a payload of freshly-forged elements back into the gas.

## What the reader does

Watch your star reach its endpoint; see which remnant it leaves and how much energy and
enriched mass it returns to the cluster. Change the birth mass and watch the fate flip
across the thresholds.
